Molokhiya Recipe

Ingredients

1

4 Cups of chicken or meat stock

2

Frozen ALGHAZAL Molokhia

3

1 Tablespoon of butter

4

5 to 6 Crushed garlic cloves

5

1 Tablespoon of dry coriander

Recipe

1

Bring 2 cups of chicken or meat stock to a boil in a heavy sauce pot.

2

Add in a diced onion and sauté until lightly brown.

3

Stir in 1/2 a kilo of meat cubes and sauté until browned.

4

Add the necessary hot water to cook them with the juice of 2 fresh tomatoes or a tablespoon of tomato concentrate.

5

Season to taste with salt and condiments as desired.

6

When meat is cooked, add frozen ALGHAZAL Okra with 5 to 6 cloves of garlic.

7

Add more hot water if needed and leave to simmer until obtaining a smooth sauce.

8

At the end of cooking, add lemon juice at will.

9

Serve with rice aside.

Okra Recipe

Ingredients

1

1 Tablespoon of butter

2

1 Diced onion

3

1/2 kilo of meat cubes

4

2 Fresh tomatoes juice or tomato concentrate

5

Salt and pepper

6

Lemon

7

5 to 6 Cloves of garlic

Recipe

1

Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.

2

Add the ALGHAZAL Frozen Molokhia while stirring from time to time until reaching complete homogeneity.

3

Season to taste with salt.

4

In a separate frying pan, heat a tablespoon of butter and fry 5 to 6 crushed garlic cloves with a tablespoon of dry coriander.

5

Stir into the soup, as it starts boiling remove immediately from the heat.

6

Serve with rice aside and fried chicken.

Spinach Recipe

Ingredients

1

2 Tablespoons of butter

2

1 Diced onion

3

1/4 Kilo of minced meat

4

Tomato paste

5

Frozen ALGHAZAL Spinach

6

5-6 Mashed cloves of garlic

7

1 Tablespoon of fresh chopped coriander leaves

Recipe

1

Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.

2

Add in a diced onion and sauté until lightly brown.

3

Stir in 1/4 kilo of minced meat, sauté until browned.

4

Add salt, tomato paste and the frozen ALGHAZAL Spinach to be cooked all together.

5

Heat a tablespoon of butter in a separate small frying pan.

6

Add 5-6 mashed cloves of garlic and a tablespoon of fresh chopped coriander leaves (Cilantro).

7

Stir immediately into the spinach. Serve with rice aside.

Fresh Orange Juice

Ingredients

1

4 large oranges

2

Ice cubes (optional)

Recipe

1

Roll the oranges on the counter with your hand to make them easier to juice.

2

Cut the oranges in half.

3

Squeeze the juice out using a citrus juicer or by hand.

4

Pour the fresh orange juice into a glass.

5

5. Add ice cubes if you like your juice chilled.

Roasted Broccoli with Garlic Recipe

Ingredients

1

1 head of broccoli, cut into florets

2

2 tablespoons olive oil

3

3 garlic cloves, minced

4

Salt and pepper to taste

5

Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Recipe

1

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

2

Toss the broccoli florets with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.

3

Spread the broccoli on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, until tender and slightly crispy.

4

Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Cauliflower Mash Recipe

Ingredients

1

1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets

2

2 tablespoons butter

3

1/4 cup milk or cream

4

Salt and pepper to taste

5

Fresh chives for garnish

Recipe

1

Steam or boil the cauliflower florets until tender, about 10-12 minutes.

2

Drain and transfer to a food processor.

3

Add butter, milk or cream, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.

4

4. Garnish with chopped chives.

Crispy French Fries

Ingredients

1

4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into fries

2

3 cups vegetable oil for frying

3

Salt to taste

4

Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or your favorite seasoning

Recipe

1

Heat the oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).

2

Fry the potatoes in batches for about 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy.

3

Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.

4

Season with salt and any additional seasoning of your choice.
