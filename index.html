ALGHAZAL, an Egyptian company founded in Egypt in the year 2018, with a vision to promote all year round accessibility of commendable frozen fruits and vegetables which are cost-effective in Egypt and the world.

Along the way, we got a chance to provide to Europe as well as other countries: the Netherlands, Serbia, Italy, Poland, Greece, Georgia, Russia, and China in addition to all the Arab countries. Through our 6 years of experience in the production sector with our farmers, operations team, and customer relationship managers, we are stepping the industry in the region. Farmers of our company collect the most robust fruits and vegetables to be quick frozen, packed, and distributed to our partners without delay.
