El-Rawda for food processing was established in Egypt in 2008 to promote all year long accessibility of high quality cost effective frozen fruits and vegetables in Egypt and around the globe.

Since that time we had the pleasure to supply to Europe countries and other countries like Netherland, Serbia, Italy, Poland, Greece, Georgia Russia and China as well as all the Arab countries. Thanks to our 15 years of experience of our farmers, operation team and customer relationship managers, we are pioneering the industry in the region. “Our farmers pick up the healthiest fruits and vegetables to be individually quick frozen, packaged and delivered to our partners in a timely manner”
