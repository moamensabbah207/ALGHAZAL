ALGHAZAL For Food Industries

Our story

ALGHAZAL. is a leading name in the frozen food industry, known for delivering premium IQF products. From the very start, we've been dedicated to mastering every step of the process—from production to packaging—to ensure our customers receive only the best.

With a wide range of frozen fruits and vegetables, ALGHAZAL. has made it our mission to provide exceptional quality that meets global standards. We're proud to supply products that not only taste great but also retain their nutritional value, consistently surpassing customer expectations.

Our history

Our products have found their way into homes and businesses across Europe and beyond. We proudly export to countries like the Netherlands, Serbia, Italy, Poland, Greece, Georgia, Russia, and China, as well as throughout the Arab world. At ALGHAZAL., we take pride in offering quality you can trust, wherever you are.
